Week 4 - Cultural Blog

Sexism in Silicon Valley

It seems like every month a story comes out of Silicon Valley that some executive got caught making a sexist comment or a former employee is pressing charges against her former employer for sexual harassment. It’s no secret that men out number women in the software industry, but there’s no excuse for harassment of any type against any employee.

DBC is doing a great service by teaching empathy to the students that attend their classes. Being able to put yourself in another person’s shoes is one of the most important attributes a human being can have. As for individuals wondering what they can do, treating other adults as your equals and leading by example will help greatly.
